Step 1: Enter Your Document

Paste your literature text into the text box in the Tools Center. Direct text input is supported. For best results, limit each submission to around 5,000 characters.

Step 2: Choose Processing Functions

Select one or more functions from the panel on the right. Free users can use Summary, Translation, and Keyword Extraction. Deep Analysis, Document Comparison, and Citation Formatting require a membership.

Step 3: Get AI Results

Click "Start Processing" and the AI will analyze your document within seconds. Members enjoy priority model access and more detailed analysis reports.

Members can export results to Word or PDF format for archiving and further editing.
